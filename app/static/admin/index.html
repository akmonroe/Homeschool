Dictation audio (TTS)

VCTK voice and playback speed for the student dictation app. Lower speed = slower, clearer (same scale as DICTATION_TTS_* in Docker: 0.25–1.0). Save applies in memory until you reset or restart the app.

1.0 = no slowdown; 0.58 ≈ default.

Master dictionary

Each card is one core.lexemes row: identifiers, definition, pronunciation / etymology / tips when present, full extensions JSON, and quick edits for level and definition. Add words via Spelling words (AI) or enrich with scripts/import_school_spelling_words.py (see docs).
